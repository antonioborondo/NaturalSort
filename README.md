# NaturalSort
C++ Implementation of Natural Comparision and Natural Sort

For Example -


	Inputs :
		Hello 100
		Hello 34
		Hello 9
		Hello 25
		Hello 10
		Hello 8

	Normal Sort Output :
		Hello 10
		Hello 100
		Hello 25
		Hello 34
		Hello 8
		Hello 9

	Natural Sort Output :
		Hello 8
		Hello 9
		Hello 10
		Hello 25
		Hello 34
		Hello 100
